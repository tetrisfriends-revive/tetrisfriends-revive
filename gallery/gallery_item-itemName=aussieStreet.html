Tetris Lighting the Way

Tweet
Source: http://technabob.com/blog/2008/12/09/tetris-hits-the-streets-somewhere-in-australia/
Tetris has been elevated as art quite literally! Through the month of January 2009, an outdoor art exhibit in Sydney, Australia showcased Tetriminos suspended over the heads of pedestrians along a narrow ally. Imagine now a real Tetris game that uses blocks that big. By our measure, the Matrix would be more than 600 feet tall!
