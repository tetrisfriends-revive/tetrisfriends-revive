  2. 2. PERSONAL INFORMATION WE COLLECT

    Top

    We may collect various types of information about you, including personal, demographic and aggregate data.

Demographic information is not considered personal information unless it is linked with your other personal information, which we may do for a variety of reasons such as to customize the content according to your interests and preferences. Personal information does not include aggregate information, which is data we collect about the use of the TOA Products or categories of such users, from which personal information has been removed. Aggregate data is used for a number of purposes such as to help us understand trends and user needs so that we can improve our products and services.

If you leave a comment on a forum or provide a testimonial on the TOA Products, you should be aware that any personally identifiable information you submit there can be read, collected, or used by other users, and could be used to send you unsolicited messages. We are not responsible for the personally identifiable information you choose to submit on or in the TOA Products.

If we collect information from you offline and it is linked with personal information, we will treat that information as personal information. Some personal information may also be automatically collected through the use of the TOA Products, as is described below (See Use of Cookies, IP Addresses and Web Beacons).

    When you use a TOA Product on your social network page or on someone else’s page, we may record information regarding the use of that TOA Product, and we may collect other information that is visible on your social network page in accordance with the relevant terms of use associated with that page.

  6. 6. USE OF COOKIES, IP ADDRESSES AND WEB BEACONS

    Top

    When you visit the TOA Products, we automatically collect certain information, such as your computer’s Internet Protocol (IP) address, your computer’s operating system, the browser type, the address of the referring Web site, and your activity on the TOA Products. We may also collect certain information through the use of cookies. Cookies are small text files that are stored on your hard drive and record your preference and other data about your visit to the TOA Products.

    If we combine cookies with or link them to any of the personally identifying information, we will treat this information as personally identifiable information; otherwise it is treated as and used in the aggregate. You may block, erase, or be warned of cookies through the browser settings on your computer; however, if a browser is set not to accept cookies or if a user rejects a cookie, you may not be able to sign in or access TOA Products or certain features of TOA Products.

    "Web beacons" (also known as "Web bugs," "action tags," "pixel trackers," or "clear GIFs") may also be used on the TOA Products or in emails relating to the TOA Products. Web beacons are small graphic images placed on a Web page or social network service application, Web-based document, or in an email message that allow the collection of certain information and monitor user activity on the TOA Products. Web beacons are invisible because of their size (only 1 by 1 pixel) and because they are the same color as the background of the Web page or application. Web beacons are used to collect non–personal information such as the IP address of the computer that the Web beacon is sent to, the URL of the page the Web user comes from and the time the Web beacon is activated (such as when you view the Web page, application, advertisement, or email that contains the Web beacon).

    We may also use one or more web analytics services. Web analytics services may record mouse clicks, mouse movements, scrolling activity as well as any text you type on any TOA Product. We may use the information collected through web analytics services to improve the user experience on TOA Products.
