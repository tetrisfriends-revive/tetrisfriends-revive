What are Missions and Badges?

Looking for an extra challenge? When you successfully complete a Mission in the designated amount of time, you’ll be awarded a custom Badge for your efforts. Badges will then be displayed on your profile page for everyone else to bask in your glory. The more Missions you successfully complete, the more Badges you’ll receive! Try to collect them all.
